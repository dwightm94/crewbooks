Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2026

Overview

CrewBooks ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly: your name, email address, phone number, company name, and business address when you create an account. We also collect job data, crew information, invoice details, expenses, and photos you upload while using the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve CrewBooks services, including: managing your jobs and crew, generating invoices, sending notifications, and processing payments. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Amazon Web Services (AWS) infrastructure in the United States. We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and follow industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate CrewBooks:

Amazon Web Services (AWS) for hosting and data storage. Amazon Cognito for authentication. Amazon SES for email delivery. These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.

Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage to keep you logged in and store your preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics.

Your Rights

You can request to view, update, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us. You can delete your account through the app settings, which will remove your data from our systems.

Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data within 30 days, except where required by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact us at crewbookss@gmail.com.
